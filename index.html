We don't collect any data. Period.

Times Table Practice runs entirely on your device. No user data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any servers.

Information We DON'T Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any information, including:

  • Personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports or performance data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any form of user data whatsoever

How Your Data Stays Private

All app data remains exclusively on your device:

  • High Scores - Stored locally on your device
  • Practice Progress - Saved only to your device's storage
  • Learning Weights - Calculated and kept on your device
  • Settings - Remain private to your device

This data cannot be accessed by us or any third party. Deleting the app removes all associated data.

No Third-Party Services

Times Table Practice does not integrate with or use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Cloud storage services
  • External APIs or backends

Children's Privacy

Times Table Practice is designed to be safe for all ages. Since we don't collect any data, children can use this app without any privacy concerns. The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements by not collecting any personal information from users of any age.

Your Privacy Choices

Since we don't collect any data, there are no privacy settings to manage or data to request. You have complete control over your information because it never leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app handles data (though we don't plan to), we will:

  • Update this privacy policy
  • Include a notice in the app update description
  • Update the "Last Updated" date above

Open Source Commitment

We believe in transparency. Our privacy practices are simple because our app is simple - focused solely on helping you master multiplication tables without any data collection.
