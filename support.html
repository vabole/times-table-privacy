‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the smart practice work?
The app monitors how quickly and accurately you answer each multiplication problem. Questions you struggle with appear more frequently, while ones you've mastered show up less often. This ensures you spend time practicing what needs the most work.
Why are some questions appearing more than others?
This is intentional! The app has learned that you need more practice with certain multiplication tables. As you improve, those questions will appear less frequently and you'll see more variety.
How can I see which problems I need to work on?
Tap the "Review" button on the home screen to see your top problematic questions. This shows which multiplication problems need the most attention based on your performance.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely! Times Table Practice doesn't collect any personal data. All your scores, progress, and learning data stays exclusively on your device. We can't see or access any of your information. Read our Privacy Policy
Can I reset my progress?
Yes! To start fresh, simply delete and reinstall the app. This will clear all stored progress and learning weights, giving you a completely clean slate.
What age group is this app designed for?
Times Table Practice is perfect for elementary students (ages 7-12) learning multiplication, but it's also great for older students who want to improve their speed and accuracy, or adults refreshing their math skills.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! Times Table Practice works completely offline. No internet connection is required for any features. Perfect for practicing anywhere, anytime.
How can I improve my scores?
Focus on accuracy first, then speed will naturally follow. Use the Review feature to identify your weakest areas and practice consistently. The app's adaptive system will help you improve by giving you more practice on challenging problems.
